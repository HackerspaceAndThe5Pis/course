Preparing CAD model for manufacturing

Notes: The instructions below outlines the process involved on using Fusion360 to prepare the cabinet for manufacturing on a CNC machine. Previously developed files may be provided, so you can use these on the machine control software to cut the pieces straightaway.

1. Firstly, you will need a CAD model of the arcade cabinet you want to cut on the CNC machine.

A CAD model file is provided CAD Files

2. Show the CNC machine on how to cut this model from your stock material.

A toolpath will be required to make on the model to define how the model will sit on the CNC and what tools the CNC will use. This process is called CAM in Fusion360.

2.1 Get to the CAM workspace by changing the space selector as seen above.

2.2 A setup is created

In the CAM workspace, you need to define what the raw material will be and how is your model oriented.
